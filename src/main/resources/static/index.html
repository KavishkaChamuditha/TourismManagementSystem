Legal requirements for overseas tourists.

  • Valid Passport: Overseas tourists must have a valid passport with a minimum validity of six months from the date of entry.

  • Most foreign nationals require a visa to enter Sri Lanka. Tourists can apply for a visa online or obtain one on arrival at the designated entry points in Sri Lanka. It is important to check the visa requirements based on your nationality before traveling.

  • ETA (Electronic Travel Authorization): Tourists can obtain an ETA online before traveling to Sri Lanka. The ETA is an entry permit that allows tourists to stay in Sri Lanka for a specific period, usually up to 30 days.

  • Vaccination Requirements: Depending on the country of origin, tourists may be required to provide proof of vaccination for certain diseases such as yellow fever. It is advisable to check the vaccination requirements before traveling.
